My Education

I have recently finished my final year of my honours degree at Edinburgh Napier University studying BEng Computer Security and Forensics.

Over the course of the four years I covered a multitude of subjects including:

  • Networked Services 
    Linux system administration including the configuration of webservers and users
  • Security Testing and Advanced Network Forensics
    Penetration and stress testing using tools such as NMAP, Metasploit and several more 
  • Distributed Services and Applications
    Creating a cloud storage service using a secret sharing encryption method
  • Information: Security and Society
    Working with security policies and frameworks, such as ISO 27000, ethics and laws
  • Honours Project
    A performance and feature analysis of virtualized firewalls
  • Applied Cisco Networking and Practical Networks 1/2
    Working heavily with Cisco based networking devices and Cisco IOS
